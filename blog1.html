How We Worked During Covid-19 pandemic

The emergence of the COVID-19 pandemic has significantly influenced the landscape of waste management strategies. People tend to steer clear of waste even under normal circumstances due to its association with disease. So needless to say managing waste during a pandemic was a real challenge. The safety of the Haritha Karma Sena (HKS) waste collectors became a central concern that demanded our attention.

To address this challenge, we equipped the HKS team with comprehensive safety measures, including gloves, sanitizers, and masks. Furthermore, they strictly adhered to social distancing protocols while in the field. The Haritha Karma Sena played a crucial role in collecting non-biodegradable waste from both residences and commercial establishments. Subsequently, these collected

materials underwent processing in shredding units to facilitate recycling. The waste underwent meticulous segregation into distinct categories based on their characteristics, encompassing P.P, H.M White, Bulb, PVC, LDPrint, Bajar, H.M, Ganny, bottles, E-waste, steel, tubes, LD(Glucose), bottle caps, spray bottles, and more.

A noteworthy feat is that each Kudumbashree worker undertook the task of visiting a minimum of 250 households to collect non-biodegradable waste, with the collection process being tied to a user fee structure. Comprehensive feedback was gathered from each household concerning the waste collection experience. The residents of Kanhangad municipality exhibited remarkable support and consideration towards the HKS team even in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic. This solidarity and cooperation are integral to the resounding success of Kanhangad's designation as a zero waste generating municipality.

Amidst the COVID-19 era, our efforts culminated in the collection of a staggering 20 lakh kilograms of plastic waste from various panchayats and institutions across Kerala. These materials were subject to scientific disposal methods, ensuring a sustainable and responsible approach to waste management.
